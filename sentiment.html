Reviews Revealed

We took a snippet of data and implemented Machine Learning to uncover insights related to Airbnb reviews!

Using gender.detector, reviews are separated into gender categories first. We then, apply vader.sentiment analysis to break down the percentage of text per gender that falls within these categories: Positive, Negative and Neutral.
We took all reviews for December 2018 and ran a word frequency analysis resulting in words with higher counts relatively bigger than those with smaller number of appearances.
